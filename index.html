About the Program

The Master's program in Digital Farming systematically prepares students to meet the challenges of smart agriculture. It blends agricultural sciences with technology, AI, robotics, and digitalisation — aiming to enhance sustainability, optimize resources, and improve animal welfare while managing the high workloads of modern farming. The program also provides international exposure and intercultural competence, preparing students for global agricultural innovation.

How it connects with my work

  • Building full-stack pipelines to collect, process, and visualize agricultural and environmental data.
  • Exploring robotics and AI-driven automation to improve precision farming.
  • Developing scalable, cloud-deployed solutions for sustainable agriculture.
